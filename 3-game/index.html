Higher Or Lower

After shuffling the deck, the game will prompt you to pick a card. The goal of the game is to go through the whole deck by guessing if the next card will be higher or lower than the card that you have picked. If you pick a card and think the next card will be higher, click the higher button. If you pick a card and think the next card will be higher, click the lower button. If you are right, that next card will be added to that pile. If you are wrong, the card will be put upside down, and you would now have lost that space. The goal is to get all of the cards in the piles without having all of the piles with a face down card.
